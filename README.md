## Expected Players Points per Possession (EPPP)
A new statistic that calculates the expected value of a single player's possession of a ball. When the ball touches your hands, how many points do you convert it into, on average? Accounts for shot selection, accuracy, foul-drawing ability, ft%, and more shooting metrics, as well as turnover probability and associated team defense.

- Tested with 2025-26 stats (as of 3/8/2026) and all-time
- Includes a Weight adjusted EPPP (WEPPP) that factors in usage, allowing star players to rise with respect to elite role players
